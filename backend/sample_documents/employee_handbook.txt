DocuMind Demo Employee Handbook

Paid Time Off
Full-time employees receive 15 days of paid time off each calendar year. PTO requests should be submitted at least two weeks before the planned absence.

Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Confidential files must remain in approved company systems.

Security
Passwords must be at least twelve characters and multifactor authentication is required for administrative tools.
